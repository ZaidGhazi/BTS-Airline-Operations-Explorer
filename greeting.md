# Welcome to the BTS Airline Operations Explorer

Use this app to explore Bureau of Transportation Statistics Airline On-Time Performance records for major U.S. airports. You can filter flights in natural language, build your own plot with X, Y, color, and geom controls, then ask the interpretation coach about the current plot.

This app supports exploratory descriptive analysis only. It can help describe patterns in delays, cancellations, routes, carriers, airports, seasons, and reported delay causes, but it is not causal proof that any one factor caused an observed change.

The field guide in the app explains useful variables such as carrier, origin, destination, route, date, season, time of day, delay minutes, cancellation flags, and delay-cause minutes.
